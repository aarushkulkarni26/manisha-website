About us

Growing up childcare is home based child care in Arlington heights. We offers warm, nurturing education and care for 2 months to 5 year old children.We provide a well-balanced curriculum in a safe environment that promotes growth and development of each individual child to meet his or her physical, emotional, social and cognitive needs. Children are very special to us and their well-being is our main concern. We truly enjoy talking and working with each child and meeting his or her physical and emotional needs. Your child will be loved and treated as uniquely special person while in our care.

Our Philosphy

We believe that a child who feels loved and accepted will establish a secure foundation and a high self-esteem.

    Our goal is to provide a safe and secure environment for your child that will foster his/her physical, social, emotional and cognitive development.
  • Develop communication and coordination skills.
  • Stimulate expression through art and skills.
  • Provides opportunities for physical development.
  • Respect each child as unique individual.
  • Help children develop respect for others.
  • Encourage appreciation of joy and value of learning.
  • Generate curiosity, initiative, creativity and independent thought.
All our teachers are certified by the state of Massachusetts Department of Early education and care.
